Courtesy of www.lemonlooks.org

Lady Lupita Lemon: An American Success Story

Lady Lemon never stops impressing us. Her most recent initiative brought economic growth to many small villages in Africa and South America. It’s not often someone of such fame is also so kind-hearted. Her kindness is relevant in every stage of her life. Lady Lemon was once a medical school student and up-and-coming surgeon. She won the Radicand Grant, a prestigious medical award in which many of the winners go on to do amazing things. Even the runners-up to the award in her year went on to have careers at hospitals such as St. Joseph’s and Harvard Medical. Lady Lemon dropped out shortly after to pursue her dream in fashion…
