Acquaint yourself with the case and the suspects before viewing the evidence.

The Backstory

The Honorable M. M. Mahogany invited five guests to his mountain estate for a weekend getaway; “A holiday” he put it. “You five are the lucky winners.” Each was given a room for their own and was allowed to use all the amenities of his estate to their pleasure. Late one evening, the local police were contacted and informed that Mahogany had died! Unfortunately, a rock fell on the narrow, winding road leading to the house and prevented the police from arriving on the scene immediately. Only digital evidence could be gathered to figure out the solution to the crime.

The Characters*

The Victim:
M. M. Mahogany has served as a local judge for over 30 years. His wife, Debora, died soon after they were married.

Suspect #1:
Mr. Lawrence Lavender is known to be elusive, but also very cunning. His only occupation seems to be traveling and intrigue.

Suspect #2:
Mrs. Andrea Aqua is a well-known billionaire and heiress to the Aqua Family Fortune. She is known to be gentle but firm.

Suspect #3:
Dr. Bethany Black is an intelligent surgeon and medical expert. She works for the renowned St. Joseph’s Medical Center.

Suspect #4:
Professor Cornelius Copper is an intense academic. He teaches at Garreth Bernard University and has multiple degrees.

Suspect #5:
Miss Lana Leaf is a bright socialite. She works for a marketing company and manages a team of nearly 100 people.

*Note that no character information carries over from other Scenarios.
