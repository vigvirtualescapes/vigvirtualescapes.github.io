The Backstory

Monsieur Mateo Midnight was a famed logician. One fine June evening he decided to have five guests over to teach them all he knew of logic; he did this out of love of the craft, or, maybe, just sheer boredom. Unfortunately, one of the guests didn’t take a liking to Monsieur Midnight’s wordy games and killed him. However, the only directions to Monsieur Midnight’s house were too confusing for the police to arrive immediately. Therefore, they had to take statements, digitally, from each of the guests. However, they had become so versed in Monsieur Midnight’s teachings that their statements were clouded in syllogistic jargon.

(NOTE: Assume that if a characters states something straightforwardly such as "I was in X when the murder occurred", then they are telling the truth)

The Characters*

The Victim:
Monsieur Mateo Midnight, a logician and aspiring sumo wrestler, prided himself in his collection of over 200 brands of staplers.

Suspect #1:
Mr. Lawrence Lavender is known to be elusive, but also very cunning. His only occupation seems to be traveling and intrigue.

Suspect #2:
Mrs. Andrea Aqua is a well-known billionaire and heiress to the Aqua Family Fortune. She is known to be gentle but firm.

Suspect #3:
Dr. Bethany Black is an intelligent surgeon and medical expert. She works for the renowned St. Joseph’s Medical Center.

Suspect #4:
Professor Cornelius Copper is an intense academic. He teaches at Garreth Bernard University and has multiple degrees.

Suspect #5:
Miss Lana Leaf is a bright socialite. She works for a marketing company and manages a team of nearly 100 people.

*Note that no character information carries over from other Scenarios.
