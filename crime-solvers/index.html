A Virtual Detective Experience

Players
1+ (max. 5 recommended)
Length
15-30 minutes
Difficulty
Varies

Crime Solvers is a virtual mystery game in which you (or you and a group of your friends) are trying to solve a murder using a set of clues and your skills of deduction.

If playing with a group (a maximum of five players is recommended), you may choose to work as a team, or make it a race to see who can solve the mystery first.

Before you begin, decide on a scenario below. If playing with a team on different devices, make sure you all click the same scenario. Each player will need access to the internet, either on a laptop (highly recommended) or a smart phone.

In each scenario, you must determine the following:

  • The murderer
  • The murder weapon
  • The time of the murder
  • The murderer's motive
  • The location of each suspect at the time of the murder

Everything else about the suspects, the scenario, etc., will be described once you launch the game. Now get solving!

Note: if autoplay is enabled in your browser, enjoy a bit of background music to complement your crime -solving experience!
