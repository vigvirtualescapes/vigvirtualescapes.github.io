The Backstory

Madam M. Mary Magenta, a retired business woman and widow of the late Major M. Methuselah Magenta, invited five guests to her suburban home for a mysterious gathering. Little to the guests knowledge, Madam Magenta is sometimes referred to as “The Matchmaker.” In fact, she was interviewing each of them as potential romantic candidates for others she knows. The guests thought this quite odd, but went along with it out of kindness towards Madam Magenta, and their own longing hearts. Unfortunately, the local police have been having some internal difficulties and weren’t able to get to the scene immediately. Only digital evidence could be gathered to figure out the solution to the crime.

The Characters*

The Victim:
Madam M. Mary Magenta, mother of M. Martha and M. Maggie, has a kind exterior, but can be stern and stubborn at times.

Suspect #1:
Mr. Lawrence Lavender is known to be elusive, but also very cunning. His only occupation seems to be traveling and intrigue.

Suspect #2:
Mrs. Andrea Aqua is a well-known billionaire and heiress to the Aqua Family Fortune. She is known to be gentle but firm.

Suspect #3:
Dr. Bethany Black is an intelligent surgeon and medical expert. She works for the renowned St. Joseph’s Medical Center.

Suspect #4:
Professor Cornelius Copper is an intense academic. He teaches at Garreth Bernard University and has multiple degrees.

Suspect #5:
Miss Lana Leaf is a bright socialite. She works for a marketing company and manages a team of nearly 100 people.

*Note that no character information carries over from other Scenarios.
